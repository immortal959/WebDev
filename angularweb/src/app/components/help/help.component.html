Help

Map: Click on the Map button in the menu to open the geoportal map.

Drawing buildings: Click the account_balance icon on the map to start drawing a polygon. Double-click to finish. You will be redirected to the Building form with the geometry pre-filled.

Drawing streets: Click the route icon to draw a linestring. Double-click to finish. You will be redirected to the Street form.

Drawing points: Click the place icon to draw a point. You will be redirected to the Point form.

Forms: Use the forms to insert, select, update and delete data from the database. Click on a row in the table to load its data into the form.

Login: You must be logged in to modify data. Use the login button in the menu.
